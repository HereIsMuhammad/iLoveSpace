# Code of Conduct

## Welcome to iLoveSpace! 

This is my personal space repository. 
Everyone is welcome to explore, learn, and have fun with space projects and resources here. 
Just be kind, respectful, and enjoy the journey!

## How to behave

### Do
- Be nice and polite.
- Ask questions if you’re curious.
- Respect other people’s ideas and work.

### Don’t
- Be mean or rude.
- Share anyone’s personal info.
- Spam or post unrelated content.

## Scope
This code of conduct is just for this repo and any discussions in issues or comments.
