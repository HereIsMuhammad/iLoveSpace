# Open Space & Universe Databases

It contains **open-source / open-access databases**
focused on **space, universe, astronomy, and cosmology**.

All links here are free to explore and use for learning, research, and projects.

## Contents
- [Open Access Journals](#open-access-space-journals)
- [Research Papers & Preprints](#research-papers--preprints)
- [Astronomical Data Archives](#space-data-archives)

## Open Access Space Journals

- **Universe (MDPI)**  
  https://www.mdpi.com/journal/universe  
  *Cosmology, astrophysics, and space science*

- **Astronomy (MDPI)**  
  https://www.mdpi.com/journal/astronomy  
  *Observational & theoretical astronomy*

- **Discover Space (Springer)**  
  https://link.springer.com/journal/11038  
  *Open access space science journal*

- **Open Access Journal of Astronomy (OAJA)**  
  https://medwinpublishers.com/OAJA/  
  *Peer-reviewed astronomy research*

## Research Papers & Preprints

- **arXiv (Astrophysics Section)**  
  https://arxiv.org/archive/astro-ph  
  *Free preprints on astronomy, cosmology & space physics*

- **NASA ADS**  
  https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/  
  *Search engine for astrophysics & space science literature*

## Space Data Archives

- **HEASARC (NASA)**  
  https://heasarc.gsfc.nasa.gov/W3Browse/  
  *High-energy astrophysics mission data*

- **VizieR Catalogue Service**  
  http://vizier.u-strasbg.fr/  
  *Massive collection of astronomical catalogs*

- **ESASky (ESA)**  
  https://www.cosmos.esa.int/web/esasky  
  *ESA space mission images & data*

- **OpenUniverse**  
  https://openuniverse.cbpf.br/  
  *Multi-wavelength astronomy data*

## Scholarly Indexes

- **OpenAlex**  
  https://openalex.org/  
  *Open index of research articles, authors, and journals*

- **Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ)**  
  https://doaj.org/  
  *Find more open access space & astronomy journals*
